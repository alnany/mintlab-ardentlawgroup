About the Firm

Experience. Preparation. Results.

A firm defined by focus and the quality of its outcomes.

Ardent Law Group®   (“Ardent”) is a boutique law firm devoted solely to the needs of businesses. We specialize in matters involving Business Litigation , Corporate/Business Transactions, Franchise , Real Estate Law , Trademark & Copyright , and Labor & Employment Law . Ardent is a multi-cultural law firm with both domestic and international clients ranging from small/medium sized emerging businesses to international conglomerates to fortune 500 corporations. We counsel companies from all over the nation and globally, including international clients in China, Korea, Taiwan and Vietnam.

Our highly trained Attorneys can effectively and efficiently handle all your legal matters involving intellectual property, patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets, unfair competition, and trade dress.

Ardent’s litigation services are intensely focused on your bottom line – we understand that when you hire an attorney or law firm, you trust the legal matter will be handled as effectively and efficiently as possible.
