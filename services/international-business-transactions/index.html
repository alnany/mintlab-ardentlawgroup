International Business Transactions

In the United States and abroad, companies and individuals can face complex issues and problems when crossing international borders. Our Attorneys have successfully represented the U.S. business interests of foreign-based companies as well as the interests of U.S.-based companies doing business in other countries. Our Attorneys have also represented individuals doing business and investing outside of the United States and foreign individuals needing assistance in doing business within the United States. With many of the firm’s Attorneys being multilingual and multicultural, we are uniquely qualified legal advisors to foreign-based businesses and individuals and to U.S.-based companies and individuals doing business in other countries. Our Attorneys have extensive experience in a variety of areas, including:

  • Setting Up Companies
  • Arranging Financing
  • Mergers & Acquisitions
  • Joint Ventures
  • Licensing
  • Negotiating and Drafting Various Agreement (e.g., Distribution and Supply Agreement, Licensing Agreement, Joint Venture Agreement, and etc.).
  • Joint Product Development
  • Intellectual Property
  • Litigation (including collections)

If you are in need of an international business transactional Attorney in the Orange County or Los Angeles County area, please call to schedule a free initial consultation.
